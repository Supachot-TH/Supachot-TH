Objectives.

The faculty’s main objectives are to produce master and doctfl graduates in the field of Information Technology, Management of Information Systems, and Data Communication & Networking in response to the demands of industrial and governmental sectors.

About

Faculty of Information Technology, King Mongkut’s University of Technology North Bangkok (KMUTNB), was started unofficially in 1996 with Master of Science Program in Information Technology (International Program) managed under the graduate college in cooperation with Monash University, Australia. In 2001 the KMUTNB university council officially declared the Faculty of Information Technology as an internal unit . The faculty has been continuously developed to offer more academic programs both International and Thai programs.
